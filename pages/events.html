The "RC CAR " Expo, organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Embed Club, held at the PACE Auditorium. The event showcased the RC car building and controlling prowess of second-year Computer Science Engineering students. The competition encompassed a Single Lane Drag Race, Track Race, and Car Sumo Combat, allowing participants to showcase their speed, control, and strategic abilities.
