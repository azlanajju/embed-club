EMBEDATHON

"Embedathon" is an inter-college event organized by P.A College of Engineering in association with Embed Club . The event aims to provide a platform for students to showcase their skills and knowledge in the field of IoT adn Embedded systems. The event typically includes a hackathon, where students from various colleges come together to develop innovative projects in embedded systems.
