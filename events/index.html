EMBERS 2023

P.A College of Engineering, Dept of CSE organizes 2 days Embedathon and project exhibition under Embed Club on 28th and 29th of April 2023. Embers is a national level technical fest which aims to solve problems and give a platform to showcase the project. Embers will be conducted at PACE campus.

Rules:

  • Students can participate as individual or team.
  • In a team there can only be 4 members.
  • Registration fee for a team is ₹ 200 irrespective of the team members count.
  • Students participating for Embedathon will have a chance to attend project competition for free.
  • Each team will have allotted space if team any other assistance they have to inform in prior.
  • Evaluation of Embedathon and project exhibition will be different.
  • Winners will be announced the second day.

Day-1 Embedathon:

Embers Embedathon is a flagship event which aims to solve real world problems mainly in 4 domains they are,

  • Agriculture
  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Environment
  • Home Automation

Rules:

  • Each team has to select any one topic from the above mentioned.
  • Open for all students.
  • Carry all the components and requirements for the event.
  • All work should be done during the Embedathon.
  • Teams can use an idea they had before the event.
  • Innovative projects are entertained.
  • Teams should stop working once the time is up, however they can do small modification on next day.
  • Breaking of rules will lead to disqualification.

Important Note:

  • Partial outputs are considered.
  • All participants will get certificate of participation.
  • Time allotted for working on the project is 9:00 am to 5:00 pm on 28th April 2023.
  • On 29th 9:00 am team should be ready for the presentation.
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.

Evaluation Criteria

    During the presentation team will be judged based on four criteria,

  • Technology
  • Design
  • Completion
  • Learning

Day-2 Project Exhibition:

Embers project exhibition is a technical event in which participant should prepare the project prior and they have to bring the project to demonstrate before the judges. There will be 2 rounds where first round will be online. Team has to provide synopsis of the project based on that will be shortlisted. Second round will be conducted in pace campus where participants have to present their project before the jury. projects can be in computer science field ( AI&ML, App, Web3 ,Block chain ,etc)which is bounded to solve real world problems

Rules:

  • Each team can have maximum 4 members.
  • B.E/PolyTechnic/BCA/B.tech students are eligible for attending the event.
  • A team member can only be part of one project.
  • Project should be presented with an effective PPT with the prototype.
  • Students can use following points to describe the project in ppt:
    1. Introduction
    2. Block Diagram
    3. Working Principle
    4. Advantages and disadvantages
    5. Future work
  • Participants should exhibit their project in the event of Embers on 29th April 2023.

Important Note:

  • Partial outputs are considered.
  • All participants will get certificate of participation.
  • Basic requirements will be made available. If any extra assistance is required member should inform prior.
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation
  • Design
  • Efficiency
  • Relevance with practical need

Note: Winners will be awarded with exciting prizes.
