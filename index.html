Mission

We are dedicated to advancing knowledge and expertise in embedded systems and IoT. Our mission is to foster a community of passionate learners and innovators who collaborate, create, and make a positive impact through technology.

About us

Welcome to the IoT and Embedded Systems Club at P A College of Engineering. We are a group of passionate students and tech enthusiasts who share a common interest in embedded systems, IoT, and computer science. Our club is dedicated to learning, collaboration, and innovation.

Our club provides a platform for students to explore, experiment, and create with technology. We organize events, workshops, and projects that allow members to gain hands-on experience and expand their knowledge in this exciting field.

Whether you're an experienced enthusiast or just starting to explore the world of embedded systems, we welcome you to join us, share your ideas, and be part of a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts.
